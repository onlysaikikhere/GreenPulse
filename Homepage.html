About GreenPulse

GreenPulse is an intelligent assistant designed to empower farmers with real-time data and insights on their soil, weather conditions, and recommended farming practices. We aim to increase farm efficiency, optimize crop yields, and promote sustainable farming practices for a greener future.

Key Features

  • Soil Analysis: Get soil data such as pH level, sand, clay, silt, and organic material density to make informed decisions.
  • Efficient Cropping Patterns: Receive suggestions for optimal crop rotations based on your soil and region.
  • Weather Forecast: Stay updated with accurate weather forecasts to manage irrigation and planting schedules.
  • Fertilizer Recommendations: Get recommendations for the best fertilizers suited to your soil conditions.

Our Mission

At GreenPulse, our mission is to leverage technology to assist farmers in achieving sustainable agriculture. By providing accessible tools and data, we hope to reduce resource waste, improve crop yields, and promote environmental stewardship.
